Sahaja Schools Newsletter | August 2023

In this moment of transition when students in the coming term may change classes, schools, countries, or even all three, we’ve put together a handy guide in the form of the most frequently asked questions that many parents have when considering sending their child to a Sahaj School. In addition, we also have another series of testimonials on Dorm Staff experiences, open vacancies at our Sahaja Schools, as well as a fresh new batch of the always entertaining quotes & stories from our students. Enjoy!

DORM STAFF AT SAHAJA SCHOOLS

There is so much that can be said about the role of the caregiver. So much so that we put together a second round of testimonials on the experience of dorm aunties and uncles in our Sahaja Schools during the year 2022/23. Listen in to hear from Emily (Canajoharie), Shobhana (DEVI), Athena and Sophia (CISS), as well as Márta (Borotin).

OUR MOST FREQUENTLY ANSWERED QUESTIONS?

Back to the basics! The first step for most children into a Sahaja School environment is at our kindergartens: ISS in the US, or Borotin and Vienna Kindergarten in Europe. The second step hopscotches to CISS for primary education, while the third leap leads to DEVI. We went to each school individually and asked the principals some questions that many parents have when considering sending their child to a Sahaj School. Here are their answers; We hope you find them enlightening!

VISION

Looking back at their year, Vienna Kindergarten has put together a beautiful video illustrating the highlights of the year, their projects on the environment, excursions in the city and beyond, including a comprehensive look at what it takes to operate a kindergarten that includes both yogis and non-yogis. The kindergarten is supported by the government, and has Sahaja Yoga as part of their pedagogy as well as being present in the curriculum. Take a look!
