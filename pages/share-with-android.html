How to share in Android App

If you would like to share an event, you have to use the share icon on the top right corner.
Once you click on the button, you will get the option to share it.
When you tap on "share", it will display the different ways you can share it.
If you go to the Transcripts, you will have the option to share a specific part of the transcript.
You can select the text you would like to share and tap on share.
Again, you will find all the ways to share it. The yogi will receive the excerpt with the link to the full lecture.
