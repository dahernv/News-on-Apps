Vimeo on Website and apps

Why Vimeo? Nowadays, the main issue with Youtube is that ads appear in some of Shri Mataji's lectures in case you don't have a paid account.

How Vimeo Works

As you can see, the controls are very similar to Youtube. But with one extra function which is very useful for Sahaja Yogis. Let's review them:
Recently, Vimeo added the option to cast videos. If you are using your desktop computer or Android App, you will have the option to cast the video through Chromecast.
If you are using your iPhone or iPad, you will find this icon to cast through your Airplay.
Just make sure you connect your device to the same Wi-Fi network.
If you click on this icon, all the subtitles available will appear on the screen.
This is a new functionality of Vimeo. When you click on this icon, you have the option to search inside the subtitle. You will see the result and when you click on it, you are directed to the specific time code of your search.
With this icon, you can select the quality of the video.
Some videos have the option to select a chapter in the video, which can give you quick access to specific parts of the video.
If you click on this, you will go to full screen mode.
