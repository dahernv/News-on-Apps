Preservation team

We have a team of Yogis located in Austria and contracted by Nirmala Vidya llc, which is dedicated to the Tape Preservation.
The task of the Tape Preservation is to primarily register, archive and safely store the collection of any physical tapes containing original Recordings of H.H. Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi, this includes the restoration of the physical tapes, of which some are torn or ripped.
Please watch the video to get to know more about the whole process.
Additional tasks of the Team is digitizing the whole variety of formats (from VHS, BETACAM till DVCpro and MiniDV), securely storing the digital files, optimizing the footage, which includes respectful Audio and Video enhancement and, finally, supporting the transcription and subtitle creation for Shri Matajiâ€™s talks.
They are continuously providing newly rendered videos to be uploaded to Amruta.
Currently three Sahaja Yogis work full time to accomplish these tasks.
