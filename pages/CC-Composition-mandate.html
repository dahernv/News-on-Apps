Dear Brothers and Sisters of world Sangha,

It is with humility and respect that we write to you to share important news from the Central Committee.
Shri Mataji, our Holy Mother, outlined the formation and mandate of the Central Committee during her discourse in Cabella after the Guru Puja on 20 July 2008. She expressed her desire for us to form a Central Committee and to communicate and attend to issues that may arise within the collective.
Guided by Her words, we recently welcomed five new members to our Central Committee:
  • Dave Dunphy, USA
  • Kasthurie Govender, South Africa
  • Maria Amelia de Kalbermatten, Switzerland/Portugal
  • Rob Henshaw, Australia
  • Victoria Zbylut, USA
We are grateful for their acceptance to serve the collective and we seek Shri Mataji's guidance through vibrations on their journey.
The continuing members of the Central Committee are
  • Didier Laurent, France
  • Hitoshi Igawa, Japan
  • Leevi Laasonen, Finland
  • Radim Ryska, Czech Republic
  • Zafar Rashid, UK/Pakistan
We would also like to thank those members who have completed their term of membership: Ann Radha Maes, Francisco Fenili, Ruth Flint, Steve Wollenberger and Volodymyr Chornyy. Their dedication and service has been invaluable.
Trying to fulfill the vision of Shri Mataji, we are here to act as a bridge to help all Sahaja Yogis and Yoginis feel connected and collective. We are committed to upholding the guiding principles of Sahaja Yoga and fostering unity within the Sangha.

May Shri Mataji continue to guide and protect us all.
With love and respect,
our brothers and sisters in the Central Committee.
