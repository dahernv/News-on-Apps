What is new on apps?

Take a look at the new version of the Nirmala Vidya App. Enjoy the new Books section. You will find:
  • The Book of Adi Shakti
  • Meta Modern Era
  • Mantra Book 8th version
  • Men and Women in Sahaja Yoga
  • Marriage in Sahaja Yoga
  • Raising Children in Sahaja Yoga
  • The Joy of Spreading Sahaja
    • Go to the footer, tap on "More...".
    You can tap on "Books".
    Each book has different formats, such as: PDF, Kindle, Mobi, Epub, which you can download or share with Sahaja Yogis.
    Also, some books have the option to buy the physical version on Lulu.
    You can listen to the "Meta Modern Era" Audiobook.
    If you read the books on apps, you will find a dropdown menu to navigate through chapters. The dropdown menu will hide while scrolling down and appears when you scroll up.
    Since there are excerpts of Shri Mataji's talks on some books such as "Raising Children", "Sahaja Marriage" and "Men and Women", you have the link to the entire lecture and the share option for those excerpts.
    Remember to visit Books section on Nirmala Vidya Amruta Website

    Check the songbook on the latest version of Nirmala Vidya app

    Go to the footer, tap on "Song Book".
    Now the search option is available on iOS.
    A side comment:
    If you search by Marathi, Hindi or Sanskrit, just enter "M 21", H 42" or "S 11". Since the songbook groups songs from different books, you may find more than one song with the same number.
    Check the new styling on the songs.
    A list of suggested bhajans for Pujas was added.
    And some excerpts of Shri Mataji about music.

    Check the Mantra Book 8th edition on apps

    Go to the footer, tap on "Mantra Book".
    Images were added to the Mantra Book.
    A direct link to Shri Mataji's talks were added anywhere in the book where a lecture is mentioned.
